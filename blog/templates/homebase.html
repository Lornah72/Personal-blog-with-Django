About

HI I'm Linda Sanga,

I am a Kenyan currently live in the US. I am a postgrad student with an interest in fashion, style, travel, photography and a creative in general. This is my little space where I share with you my world view. Grab a cup of tea, coffee, wine or whatever you like and join me in this journey. I hope to dive into more of the challenges as well as fun moments I’ve been through so far.
