Travel

As the swahili saying goes kutembea kwingi kuona mengi or as I would translate traveling the world opens up your mindset. Having the opportunity to travel is one of the best gifts you’ll ever give yourself, the interactions with different cultures, different vibe, different food, other One of the most interesting things I’ve learnt so far is that we all go through the same experiences just in a different way that influences the way we think and interact with the world. I hope to dive into more of the challenges as well as fun moments I’ve been through so far.

Fashion

I grew up enjoying putting clothes together and I also watched my mum always slay, never stepping out of the house looking shabby. I think that developed my interest in fashion and my personal style. I did a couple of beauty shows in high school and in uni as well. I also learned how to find out what fits me best, with clothes and accessories by experimenting with color, patterns, shapes and materials. I enjoy shopping for unique pieces that are both timeless and trendy.
